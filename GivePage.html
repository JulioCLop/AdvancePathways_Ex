About Advance Pathways

Helping Aurora move forward—one gift, one guest, one step at a time.

The Navigation Campus unites housing, recovery, and work under one roof so motivated adults can rebuild stability with dignity, accountability, and opportunity.

  • 255-room hotel converted into a multi-tiered campus.
  • Coordinated housing, recovery, and workforce under one roof.
  • Built so motivated adults can move up a clear pathway.

Vehicle Donation

Donating your vehicle powers pathways to recovery that build self-esteem, dignity, and independence for people experiencing homelessness.

Advance Pathways helps individuals focus on recovery from harmful behaviors while gaining livable-wage employment to move into independent housing. We focus on the whole-person approach to rebounding from homelessness and achieving the highest level of self-sufficiency.

  • Clear title and keys are all you need. We accept cars, trucks, and motorcycles—any condition.
  • Free towing is provided, and your donation is tax-deductible.
  • 80% of net proceeds support Advance Pathways; 20% supports Step Denver’s mission.
  • You may claim a charitable deduction for the gross sale of your vehicle. Please consult your tax professional.
