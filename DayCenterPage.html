Day Center

A safe daytime hub with services that move fast.

Welcoming adults 18+ for shelter from the elements, essentials, connection to care, and a clear path into longer-term housing and recovery options.

Call for details Email the team

What we offer daily

Essential services guests feel on arrival, with rapid links to deeper programs.

Day shelter & climate relief

Indoor rest, water, restrooms, and storage for belongings during open hours.

Meals & hydration

Snacks, hydration, and rotating meal support from partners and volunteers.

Navigation & referrals

On-site navigators to connect guests to housing, medical, recovery, and workforce pathways.

Charging & connectivity

Device charging, Wi-Fi, and help with online forms, benefits, and appointment scheduling.

Important

Aurora Resource Day Center update

The Aurora Resource Day Center located at 13387 E. 19th Place will be permanently closed starting November 17, 2025. Please visit the Navigation Campus Day Center going forward.

Services we provide

We welcome unhoused adults with access to:

  • Clothing, hygiene items, showers, and laundry
  • Lunch
  • Mail and document retention
  • Peer support
  • Housing assistance
  • Programming to support recovery and employment
  • Computer lab

Who we serve

Adults 18+; pets welcome if leashed and with owner.

Pets

Leashed pets may stay with owners in the Day Center; dogs only for overnights currently.

Getting here

Transit and transportation options to reach the Day Center quickly.

  • Limited van service during peak hours.
  • RTD 42 stops on 40th Ave; 121 is a short walk.
  • On-site bike racks; limited vehicle parking.

What to expect on arrival

A short check-in and immediate access to essentials.

Friendly check-in

Staff greet you, review safety guidelines, and answer questions.

Essentials

Snacks, hydration, restrooms, and a place to sit indoors.

Navigation

Optional chat with a navigator to plan housing, recovery, or workforce steps.
