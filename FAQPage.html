Safety, privacy, and access

How we keep guests, staff, and partners safe.

Safety

24/7 security, controlled entry, guest agreements, and wellness checks. We coordinate with Aurora partners for crisis support when needed.
We follow HIPAA guidance for health information and restrict photo/recording on campus. Data sharing with partners happens only with written consent.
Visitors are not allowed.
Visits are scheduled through staff and limited to common areas. Volunteers and partners check in at the welcome center and receive a badge for their shift.

Accessibility

Yes. Elevators, accessible rooms, and adaptive equipment are available. We also collaborate with PT/OT partners when mobility devices are needed.
Bilingual staff and translation services are available for intakes and key coaching sessions. Let us know language needs in advance so we can schedule coverage.
