About Advance Pathways

Helping Aurora move forward — one step, one tier, one life at a time.

The Aurora Regional Navigation Campus, inside the former Crowne Plaza at East 40th Avenue and Chambers Road, now holds 255 rooms redesigned for stability, recovery, and work. Every floor is purpose-built for dignity, accountability, and opportunity.

  • 255-room hotel converted into a multi-tiered campus.
  • Coordinated housing, recovery, and workforce under one roof.
  • Built so motivated adults can move up a clear pathway.

Our Mission Moving Forward

Advance Pathways exists to remove barriers, restore dignity, and build permanent independence.

This campus is more than a shelter — it is a launchpad where every guest can expect safety, respect, opportunity, and a real path forward.

We are honored to serve Aurora and those ready to begin again. Together, we create pathways — and advance lives.

Why now

Aurora asked for a coordinated campus. Advance Pathways is delivering it.

Our Stabilization Center, tiered dorms, and sober living homes meet emergency needs within hours. From there, guests enter Workbridge janitorial and facilities training, Pride Initiative courses, CrossPurpose and Change Please cohorts, or the upcoming C.H.E.F. culinary academy.

Arapahoe/Douglas Works! coaches now meet with guests onsite for resumes, assessments, and job placement. Donations keep those partners on campus, cover vehicle runs across Aurora, and ensure every guest can choose recovery with dignity.

“Because of Advance Pathways, I didn’t just get sober—I graduated Workbridge, reunited with my kids, and started mentoring the next cohort.” — Maya, Alumni & Workbridge graduate

Radical transparency

Radical transparency: from your gift to a guest’s first paycheck.

Every dollar is tracked. Within 72 hours you’ll see where it landed, and by day 30 you’ll see the path to that first paycheck.

First 48 Hours

Guests enter the Stabilization Center, meet their navigator, and settle into a staffed dorm or sober living home.

Days 3-14

Structured recovery agreements, Pride Initiative classes, and health appointments create rhythm and accountability.

Days 15-60

Workbridge, CrossPurpose, Change Please, and Arapahoe/Douglas Works! deliver coaching, credentials, and employer introductions.

Days 60-120

Guests secure permanent housing, join C.H.E.F. or alumni mentorship, and donors receive the full story of their investment.
